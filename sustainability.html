Sustainability

At WENO, we believe that true comfort should never come at the cost of our planet. That’s why we’re committed to crafting sofas with care — using responsibly sourced materials, eco-friendly fabrics, and sustainable production methods. From reducing waste in our workshops to designing long-lasting furniture, every step we take is guided by respect for the environment. Our goal is simple: to create comfort that feels good today and does good for tomorrow.

Eco-Friendly Materials

We carefully select sustainable materials — from responsibly sourced wood to recyclable fabrics — ensuring that every WENO sofa is both durable and kind to the environment.

Waste Reduction

Nothing goes to waste in our workshop. We reuse, recycle, and repurpose leftover materials to minimize our environmental footprint and promote a cleaner production process. deceased
