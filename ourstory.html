Our Story

WENO began with a simple idea — to make every home a place of true comfort and timeless style. It all started when a small group of furniture enthusiasts noticed that many sofas on the market looked good but lacked lasting quality and coziness. Driven by passion, they decided to create something better.




The beginning


From a tiny workshop filled with creativity and determination, WENO was born. Every sofa we design is made to bring warmth, relaxation, and a touch of modern beauty into people’s homes. Today, WENO continues to grow with one promise at heart — to craft sofas that don’t just fill a space, but complete it.

Our Journey Forward

As WENO grew, so did our dream. What began as a small workshop soon turned into a passionate team of designers, craftsmen, and dreamers — all working toward one vision: to redefine comfort and design for every home.

" We believe a sofa is more than furniture; it’s where families gather, where laughter is shared, and where memories are made. That belief guides every step we take — from choosing sustainable materials to ensuring every stitch reflects our commitment to quality. "

FOUNDER

WENO was founded by Fabiano Weno, a passionate dreamer who believed that comfort and design should live in harmony. With a deep appreciation for craftsmanship and modern living, Leap set out to create sofas that blend elegance, durability, and warmth. What began as a small idea in his hometown soon grew into a vision — to bring families closer through furniture that feels like home. Guided by creativity and care, Leap continues to lead WENO with a simple mission: to turn everyday spaces into places of comfort, connection, and inspiration.
